Astronomy

Astronomy is the study of the universe and the objects in it, including planets, stars, galaxies, and other celestial bodies.

Interesting Facts About Astronomy

  • The universe is estimated to be around 13.8 billion years old.
  • There are over 100 billion galaxies in the observable universe.
  • The largest known galaxy is called IC 1101, and it is over 6 million light-years in diameter.
  • The closest star to Earth is the Sun, which is about 93 million miles away.
  • The Moon is the Earth's only natural satellite.
